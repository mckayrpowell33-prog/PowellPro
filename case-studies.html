Anonymized case studies

These scenarios are based on typical operations challenges and example targets, not guaranteed results.

Real estate lead follow-up automation

Problem: Leads arrived from multiple sources and were followed up inconsistently.

Solution: Unified intake, lead routing, and a follow-up sequence tied to the CRM.

Typical outcomes / example targets: Reduce response time to under 10 minutes and improve lead coverage by 30–40%.

Inbox + scheduling assistant

Problem: A small business lost time to inbox triage and rescheduling requests.

Solution: AI-assisted categorization, templated responses, and scheduling automation.

Typical outcomes / example targets: Save 4–6 hours per week and reduce missed appointments.

Spreadsheet reporting + weekly summary

Problem: Weekly performance reporting required manual cleanup and copy-paste updates.

Solution: Clean data pipeline with automated rollups and executive summaries.

Typical outcomes / example targets: Cut reporting time by 60% and improve visibility across the team.
