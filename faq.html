Frequently asked questions

If you have a question not covered here, reach out and we will respond quickly.

Do I need to replace my CRM?

No. We integrate with what you already use and remove manual tasks around it.

What tools do you work with?

Common tools include Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, HubSpot, Follow Up Boss, Airtable, Calendly, Zapier, and spreadsheets.

Is my data safe?

We follow least-access principles, document data flows, and use only approved integrations.

How long does it take?

Most projects run 2–6 weeks depending on scope and access readiness.

Do you provide support?

Yes. We offer monthly support retainers for monitoring, updates, and optimization.

Can you work with my existing website?

Yes. We connect to your website forms and route data to the right systems.

What’s the difference between ChatGPT and what you build?

ChatGPT is a tool; we build structured workflows with guardrails, data connections, and measurable outputs.

Do I need technical staff?

No. We handle setup and provide clear documentation and training for your team.

Can you automate client communication?

Yes. We set up templates, approval steps, and timing rules to keep messaging consistent.

How do you measure success?

We track response times, time saved, error reduction, and adoption metrics tied to your goals.
